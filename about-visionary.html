About the Visionary

Apostle & Pastor

Apostle Kenneth M. Tuyia

Pastor of Deliverance Church Powerhouse, Nairobi

A Kenyan born-again Christian, Apostle, and revered teacher of the Word who skillfully integrates academic excellence with spiritual insight.

Ministry & Professional Background

Ministry Leadership

Apostle Kenneth M. Tuyia serves as the Pastor of Deliverance Church Powerhouse, Nairobi. He is an astute conference preacher, televangelist, motivational speaker, and life coach who has touched thousands through crusades, outreaches, worship experiences, and his service as a Christian Union Patron.

Media Ministry

Featured on prominent platforms including Books and Blogs (KTN TV Home), Big Testimony (Truth FM), and hosted the Chemichemi ya Uzima Show (Sifa FM Radio, Lamu). His ministry reaches far and wide through various media channels.

Global Outreach

He has travelled widely, both locally and internationally, proclaiming the Gospel of Christ. His ministry extends beyond borders, bringing hope and transformation to communities worldwide.

Academic Excellence

A seasoned computer scientist with international certifications from CISCO Networking Academy (USA), AFRALTI (Kenya), and ICDL (Ireland). Over 15 years of teaching experience in ICT across Kenyan institutions.

Recognition & Achievements

Holds a Certificate in Theology from Re-Forma Bible School (South Africa). Gained international recognition from Pathfinder International (USA) for excellence in HIV/AIDS awareness campaigns.

Personal Life

Joyfully married to Pastor Milcah Tali, and together they are blessed with two children, Prince and Prosper. A loving family man who leads by example in both ministry and home life.

Visionary's Mission

The mission of Apostle Kenneth M. Tuyia is to lead individuals into a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ through the power of the Holy Spirit, equipping them to live victorious lives marked by faith, purpose, and transformation.

He is dedicated to preaching the Gospel with clarity and conviction, fostering spiritual growth, and empowering believers to fulfill their God-given destinies. Through his ministry, he aims to impact communities positively by promoting holistic development—spiritually, emotionally, and socially—while upholding the values of love, integrity, and service.

Ministry Programs

Digital Generation Foundation

Established 2024

Digital Generation Foundation is a non-profit organization established by the ministry to harness the power of technology in advancing Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives. Our primary mission is to reach and uplift the less privileged through impactful programs that demonstrate God's love in action.

Jabez Global Prayer Network

Founded 2023

Jabez Global Prayer Network is a global virtual prayer movement with the mission of raising a worldwide prayer army dedicated to transforming lives and nations through the power of prayer. We are a community of believers united by faith, sharing the Word of God, prayer requests, and testimonies.

Literary Works

A prolific writer, Apostle Tuyia continues to impact lives globally through his transformative books that combine biblical wisdom with practical insights.

Seed of God

This book explores the concept of faith as a seed that, when planted in the heart, has the potential to grow into a life of abundance and blessings. It delves into biblical principles of sowing and reaping, encouraging readers to cultivate a mindset of generosity and trust in God's provision.

Healing of the Heart

In this transformative work, Apostle Tuyia addresses the emotional and spiritual wounds that individuals may carry. The book offers insights into the healing power of God's love, forgiveness, and grace, guiding readers on a journey towards inner restoration and wholeness.

Kingdom Keys

This book provides practical teachings on how to unlock the blessings and favor of God in one's life. It covers topics such as prayer, faith, obedience, and spiritual authority, equipping readers with the tools they need to navigate life's challenges and walk in victory.

A Crown Of Honor

In this inspiring book, Apostle Tuyia explores the biblical concept of honor and its significance in the life of a believer. The book encourages readers to pursue a life of integrity, humility, and service, highlighting the rewards that come from living a life that honors God and others.

Bible Study Simplified

This practical guide offers readers a straightforward approach to studying the Bible effectively. It provides tips and techniques for understanding scripture, applying biblical principles to daily life, and deepening one's relationship with God through consistent study and reflection.

True Witness

This compelling book shares testimonies of faith, miracles, and divine encounters that highlight the power of God at work in the lives of believers. Apostle Tuyia encourages readers to be bold witnesses for Christ, sharing their own stories of transformation and God's faithfulness.
