Our Community

Discover more about what makes Powerhouse Center special.

At Powerhouse Center, our community stands as the vibrant heartbeat of our mission, embodying the principles of faith, empowerment, and fellowship that define our vision. Founded by visionary leader Kenneth M. Tuyia, we bring together individuals from all walks of life—families, young professionals, students, and seekers—who share a common desire for spiritual growth, personal development, and meaningful connections.

Our community is not just a gathering place; it's a supportive network where members uplift one another through shared experiences, mutual encouragement, and collective action.

Our Community

We host a variety of engaging activities designed to strengthen bonds and inspire transformation. Weekly fellowship meetings provide opportunities for worship, prayer, and open discussions on life's challenges, drawing from our Statement of Faith that emphasizes the Holy Trinity, the divinity of Jesus Christ, and the guiding power of the Holy Spirit. Educational workshops and seminars cover topics ranging from biblical studies and leadership development to practical life skills, empowering participants to apply their faith in everyday contexts. Outreach programs extend our reach beyond our walls, involving community service projects that address local needs, from food drives and youth mentorship to environmental initiatives, all rooted in our commitment to love and service.

What truly sets our community apart is the sense of belonging and inclusivity we cultivate. Newcomers are warmly welcomed with mentorship programs and small group gatherings that foster deep, lasting relationships. Our events, including seasonal celebrations, cultural exchanges, and recreational outings, create joyful moments that reinforce our motto of unity and growth. Through these interactions, members discover their potential, overcome obstacles, and contribute to positive change, both personally and within the broader society.

Joining Powerhouse Center means becoming part of a powerhouse of hope and resilience. Whether you're looking for spiritual renewal, lifelong friendships, or a platform to make a difference, our community offers a nurturing space where faith intersects with action. Together, we build a legacy of inspiration, support, and transformation that echoes our founder's vision for a world empowered by vision and faith.
